Apna Expected Score Kaise Calculate Karein

Solved paper download karne ke baad, simple formula use karo:

Score = (Correct Answers × 4) – (Wrong Answers × 1)

Example: Agar aapke 150 correct hain aur 20 wrong, toh score = (150×4) – (20×1) = 600 – 20 = 580 marks. Apna score nikalne ke baad, Beyond Topper ke Rank & College Predictor par jaake dekho ki aapko kaunsa medical college mil sakta hai.
