Physics Answer Key Se Apni Galtiyan Kaise Pehchaane

Physics me galtiyan 3 type ki hoti hain. Answer key check karte waqt har galti ko classify karo:

1. Conceptual Mistakes

Concept hi galat samjha tha. Ye sabse serious hain — inhe theory wapas padhke fix karo. Example: direction of force, sign convention, ya kisi law ka misapplication.

2. Calculation Mistakes

Concept sahi tha lekin calculation me galti. Ye "silly mistakes" sabse zyada marks khaate hain. Solution: steps clearly likho, jaldbaazi mat karo.

3. Formula Mistakes

Galat formula laga diya ya formula bhool gaye. Solution: ek formula sheet banao aur roz revise karo.

Har galti ko classify karne se aapko pata chalta hai ki aapki asli weakness kahan hai — theory, practice, ya memory.

Physics Ko Strong Banane Ki Strategy

Bahut students Physics se darte hain, lekin sahi approach se ye scoring ban sakti hai:

  • NCERT + concept clarity: Pehle theory samjho, fir numericals.
  • Formula derivation: Sirf formula yaad mat karo, derive karna seekho — toh bhoolega nahi.
  • Daily numericals: Roz 15-20 numericals. Physics practice se aati hai, padhne se nahi.
  • Previous year questions: NEET Physics me PYQ pattern repeat hota hai.
