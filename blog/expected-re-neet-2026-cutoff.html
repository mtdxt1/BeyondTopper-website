Cutoff Ke 2 Types: Pehle Ye Samjho

Bahut students "cutoff" word se confuse hote hain. Actually do alag cheezein hain:

  1. Qualifying Cutoff (Percentile): Sirf NEET "pass" karne ke liye minimum percentile. General ke liye 50th percentile, reserved ke liye 40th. Ye marks me kam hote hain.
  2. Admission Cutoff (Marks/Rank): Kisi specific college me seat paane ke liye zaroori actual marks/rank. Ye qualifying se kaafi zyada hota hai.

Yaani sirf qualify karna kaafi nahi — achhe college ke liye admission cutoff cross karna padta hai.

RE-NEET 2026 Cutoff Ko Kya-Kya Affect Karta Hai

  • Paper difficulty: Tough paper → cutoff neeche; easy paper → cutoff upar.
  • Number of candidates: Zyada students → zyada competition → higher cutoff.
  • Seats available: Zyada seats → cutoff thoda relax.
  • Category & quota: Reserved categories aur state quota me cutoff alag hota hai.
  • Reservation policy: EWS, PwD, etc. ke alag cutoffs.

State Quota vs All India Quota — Bada Farak

Cutoff samajhne me ye distinction crucial hai:

  • All India Quota (AIQ) — 15%: Pure desh ke students compete karte hain. Cutoff zyada high hota hai. MCC counselling karwati hai.
  • State Quota — 85%: Sirf us state ke domicile students. Cutoff aksar AIQ se neeche hota hai, isliye apne home state me chance zyada ho sakti hai.

Iska matlab — agar aapke marks AIQ cutoff se kam hain, tab bhi apne state quota me achha college mil sakta hai. Dono options zaroor explore karo.

Tie-Breaking: Same Marks Pe Rank Kaise Decide Hoti Hai

Agar do students ke same marks aaye toh NTA tie-breaking rules use karti hai (priority order me):

  1. Biology me zyada marks waale ko higher rank.
  2. Fir Chemistry me zyada marks waale ko.
  3. Fir kam wrong answers (better accuracy) waale ko.

Yahi reason hai ki Biology aur accuracy par focus karna itna important hai. Amit Sir ki Biology specialisation isi me aapki madad karti hai — NEET Biology Notes dekho.

Cutoff Ke Baad: Counselling Steps

Qualify karne ke baad, MBBS/BDS seat ke liye counselling hoti hai:

  1. MCC (All India Quota) counselling — mcc.nic.in par registration.
  2. State counselling — apne state ke quota ke liye alag se.
  3. Choice filling, seat allotment, document verification, reporting.

Counselling me chhoti si galti seat chhudwa sakti hai. Beyond Topper aapko step-by-step guide karta hai — WhatsApp par counselling guidance lo.
