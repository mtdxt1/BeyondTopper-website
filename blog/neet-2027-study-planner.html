Kitne Ghante Padhna Chahiye?

Sabse common galat sawaal — "kitne ghante?" Sahi sawaal hai — "kitni quality?" Guide:

  • Class 11/12 (school ke saath): 4–6 focused hours.
  • Droppers: 8–10 focused hours, proper breaks ke saath.

10 ghante distracted padhai se 5 ghante focused padhai behtar hai. Quality > quantity, hamesha.

Subject Balance: Sabse Bada Mistake

Bahut students apne favourite subject ko zyada time dete hain aur weak subject ko ignore karte hain — ye sabse bada blunder hai. Planner me teeno subjects ko balanced time milta hai:

  • Biology: Sabse zyada marks (360), isliye daily time.
  • Chemistry: Scoring, regular NCERT revision.
  • Physics: Tough lage toh zyada practice, kam mat karo.

Revision: 40% Time Yahin Jaana Chahiye

Naya padhna jitna important hai, utna hi revision. Bina revision ke aap padha hua bhool jate ho. Planner me spaced revision built-in hai — har topic 1 din, 1 hafte, 1 mahine baad revise hota hai. Biology Notes revision ke liye perfect hain.

Test Plan: Planner Ka Sabse Important Part

Padhai + test = real progress. Planner me weekly aur monthly test schedule diya hai. NEET 2027 Test Papers use karke is schedule ko follow karo. Har test ke baad analysis karna mat bhoolna.

Planner Follow Karne Ke 5 Rules

  1. Realistic raho: Aisa plan banao jo aap actually follow kar sako.
  2. Roz review karo: Raat ko 5 min me dekho kya complete hua.
  3. Breaks lo: Bina break ke productivity girti hai.
  4. Sleep mat compromise karo: 6–7 ghante neend zaroori hai memory ke liye.
  5. Consistency > intensity: Ek din 14 ghante se behtar hai roz 8 ghante.
