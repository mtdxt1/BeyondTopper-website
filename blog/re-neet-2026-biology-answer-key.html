Botany vs Zoology: Dono Ko Alag Approach Do

Botany Verification

Botany me Plant Physiology aur Genetics se sabse zyada questions aate hain. Answer key check karte waqt diagram-based questions par extra dhyan do — leaf structure, flower parts, plant tissues. Inme labels confuse karte hain.

Zoology Verification

Zoology me Human Physiology rank decider hai. Heart, nephron, neuron, digestive system — in diagrams se direct questions aate hain. Answer key se verify karo ki aapne process steps sahi samjhe the.
