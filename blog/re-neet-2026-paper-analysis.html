RE-NEET 2026 Me "Good Score" Kya Hai?

Bahut students poochte hain "kitne marks acche hain?" Simple guide:

  • 650+ → Excellent. Government MBBS ke liye strong position.
  • 600–650 → Very good. Kaafi acche colleges possible.
  • 550–600 → Good. Category aur state quota ke hisaab se options khulte hain.
  • 500–550 → Decent. Private/state seats par focus karo.

Yaad rakho — "good score" relative hota hai. Agar paper tough tha, toh kam marks par bhi achhi rank mil sakti hai. Isiliye expected cutoff ke saath apne marks ko compare karna zaroori hai.

Paper Difficulty Cutoff Ko Kaise Affect Karti Hai

Ye concept samajhna bahut important hai:

  • Tough paper → kam students high score karte hain → cutoff neeche aata hai → kam marks par bhi achhi rank.
  • Easy paper → zyada students high score karte hain → cutoff upar jaata hai → har mark zaroori.

Isliye sirf apne marks dekhke khush ya udaas mat ho — pura context dekho. Beyond Topper ki analysis aapko ye context degi.

Time Management Analysis: Kaha Time Lagta Hai

180 questions, 200 minutes — yaani har question par average ~66 seconds. Lekin smart students aise distribute karte hain:

  • Biology (90 Q): ~50–60 min — fast, kyunki zyada direct hote hain.
  • Chemistry (45 Q): ~40–45 min — moderate.
  • Physics (45 Q): ~60–70 min — sabse zyada, numerical heavy.
  • Revision/buffer: ~15–20 min.
