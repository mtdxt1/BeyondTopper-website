NEET 2027 Ke Liye Test Practice Strategy (Amit Sir Ka Plan)

1. Chapter Khatam → Turant Test

Jaise hi koi chapter complete ho, uska chapter-wise test do. Concept fresh rehta hai aur weak areas turant pata chalte hain.

2. Har Hafte Ek Full Mock

Preparation year me kam se kam 1 full-length mock per week. Final months me 2–3 per week. Mock se stamina aur time-management dono improve hote hain.

3. Test Se Zyada Important — Analysis

Sirf test dena kaafi nahi. Har test ke baad: kitne silly mistakes, kaunse topics weak, kahan time waste hua — ye analyse karo. Yahi cheez topper aur average student me farak laati hai.

4. PYQs Zaroor Solve Karo

Previous year questions se aapko real NEET ka level aur pattern samajh aata hai. Inhe bar-bar solve karo.

Galtiyon Ka Analysis: Topper Banne Ka Asli Raaz

Sun lo dhyan se — test dena 30% kaam hai, analysis 70% hai. Har test ke baad ye 4 cheezein note karo:

  1. Silly mistakes: Aate the lekin galat kiye. Inhe minimise karna sabse zyada marks deta hai.
  2. Conceptual gaps: Topic clear nahi tha. Wapas NCERT padho.
  3. Time traps: Kaunse questions me zyada time gaya.
  4. Guessing pattern: Negative marking se kitna nuksaan hua.

Ek "error notebook" banao — har galti note karo. Exam se pehle sirf yahi notebook revise karna bahut powerful hota hai.

Negative Marking Se Kaise Bachein

NEET me –1 negative marking rank kha jaati hai. Smart rule: agar aap 2 options confidently eliminate kar sakte ho, toh attempt karna usually worth hai. Agar bilkul idea nahi, toh chhodna behtar. Test papers practice se aapko apna "risk sense" develop hota hai.

Class 11 vs Class 12 vs Dropper — Alag Approach

  • Class 11: Foundation strong banao. Chapter-wise tests par focus, jaldi full mocks me mat lago.
  • Class 12: Class 11 + 12 dono ka balance. Subject-wise tests aur monthly full mocks.
  • Droppers: Pura focus full-length mocks + PYQs + weak area improvement par.
